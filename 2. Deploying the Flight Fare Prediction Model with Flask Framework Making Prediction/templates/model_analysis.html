Model Performance Insights

Key Findings:
  • The model achieves an R² score of 87.5%, indicating strong predictive performance.
  • Total stops and airline choice are among the most important features affecting flight prices.
  • Departure and arrival times also significantly impact pricing.
  • Route (source to destination) plays a crucial role in determining flight costs.
Model Interpretation:

The XGBoost algorithm works by combining multiple weak prediction models (decision trees) to create a strong predictor. It's particularly effective for tabular data like flight pricing information.

Data Preprocessing:

Before training, categorical variables like airline names and routes were converted to numerical representations using one-hot encoding. Date and time features were extracted to capture seasonal and temporal patterns.
